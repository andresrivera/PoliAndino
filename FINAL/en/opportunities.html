Excel teaching

Este curso está diseñado para aquellos que desean adquirir habilidades básicas en el uso de esta poderosa herramienta de hoja de cálculo. En este curso, te enseñaremos los conceptos fundamentales de Excel, desde la creación y formato de hojas de cálculo hasta el uso de fórmulas y funciones para realizar cálculos y análisis de datos.

Bakery

In this pastry course you will learn to identify what tools and utensils are used to practice this profession, for the preparation of various recipes. In this baking course you will learn to:

  1. Know the basic fundamentals of baking.
  2. Understand the processes associated with preparing, designing and decorating different types of cakes.
  3. Understand the processes associated with preparing, designing and decorating different types of cookies.

File Manager

Our File Manager teaching course is designed to teach you the best practices and techniques for organizing, classifying and storing digital and physical documents effectively. During this course, you will learn to use specialized tools, techniques and software for file management, as well as implement organizational strategies that will allow you to save time and minimize the risk of losing important information..

Food handling

In our course, we invite you to explore and learn the skills necessary to handle food safely and effectively. Designed specifically for mothers and female heads of household, this course will give you the knowledge and confidence to ensure the health and well-being of your family and/or potential clients.

We will teach you the basics of food handling, from proper selection and storage to the preparation and hygiene necessary to avoid foodborne illnesses.
