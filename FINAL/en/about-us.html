About M.O.M.

We are a non-governmental organization committed to helping mothers who are heads of households. We are proud to provide them with training in various areas and connect them with job opportunities. We consider ourselves a dedicated and passionate team that strives to empower women and support them on their path to a better life. Our main objective is to provide them with the necessary tools and resources so that they can develop new skills to achieve their goals and find economic stability.

At MOM we are interested in the well-being of women heads of household and their families.
